About

The objective of the DECISION-MAP project is to systematically identify, organize, and visualize evidence addressing mental health and addictions interventions for people with acquired brain injury. Our mission is to provide a dynamic, interactive, and accessible platform for knowledge users to locate and engage with up-to-date research findings that can be used to aid in evidence-based decisions, such as treatment planning, program development, research agenda-setting, or resource allocation.
